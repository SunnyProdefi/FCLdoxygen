\subsubsection*{template$<$typename S\+\_\+$>$\newline
class fcl\+::\+R\+S\+S$<$ S\+\_\+ $>$}

A class for rectangle swept sphere bounding volume. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classfcl_1_1RSS}{R\+SS}} defines a rectangle swept sphere. The rectangle is defined in the frame T. It has one corner positioned at the origin of frame T. It lies on T\textquotesingle{}s z = 0 plane. Its longest dimension extends in the +x direction, and its shorter dimension in the +y direction.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classfcl_1_1RSS}{R\+SS}} is then posed in another frame F. R\+\_\+\+FT (the \mbox{\hyperlink{classfcl_1_1RSS_a00b547cea376bf022b6f3503e282b143}{axis}} field) is the relative orientation between frame T and F and p\+\_\+\+Fo\+To\+\_\+F (the \mbox{\hyperlink{classfcl_1_1RSS_ab37055460bfdeab00299eb57d5f567da}{To}} field) is the position of T\textquotesingle{}s origin in frame F.
